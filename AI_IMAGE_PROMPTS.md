## GENERATION TIPS

- **Platform:** Meta AI, DALL-E 3, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion
- **Style:** Maintain consistency - all should be modern, clean, professional
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher for best quality
- **Format:** JPG for photos, use PNG with transparency for graphics
- **Tone:** Tech-forward, minimalist, professional, futuristic

Once you generate the images and upload them, I'll update all the components to display them!
